Stocks

Overview of all stocks in Torn

Description of Headings

    All Stocks:

    List of all stocks

    My Blocks:

    Currently owned stocks

    Less than (<) 1%:

    Stocks with less than 1% of shares currently available

    No Shares:

    Stocks with no shares currently available

    High Demand:

    Stocks with a high interest in being purchased

    Low Demand:

    Stocks with a low interest in being purchased

    Good Forecast:

    Stocks expected to perform positively

    Poor Forecast:

    Stocks expected to perform negatively

Clicking on Stock Icons

When selecting each stock icon, an overview of the following will appear:

  • Company acronym
  • Company director
  • Stock price
  • Market cap
  • Total shares
  • Available shares
  • Forecast
  • Demand
  • Expected benefit when holding minimum indicated amount of shares
  • Current price for the benefit block
