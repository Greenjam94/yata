Give permission to the YATA bot

Some features of the YATA bot [see next section] require your personnal API key (stored in YATA) to pull information. It will not do it without your consent. The discord server owner will nerver have a direct access to your key and will only be able to use the bot functions. But, server owners can play with the roles and the permissions as they which. If they have malicious intents they can use the bot without your knowledge.
So, before giving permission to the bot, make sure that YOU TRUST THE OWNERS of every servers you're in and that are running the official YATA bot.

Bot features asking for your key

Feature 1. Stock sharing

If you happen to have the @wssb or @tcb role you're concerned. With your permission, the bot will be able to pull education time left and bank investement time left, respectively, and will make it visible to members of the server (supposed to be shared with @wssb and @tcb members).

More might come

But for now stock sharing is the only one.

Rule of thumb:

  1. Look on your servers if the official YATA bot is running
  2. Look if you got any of these roles: @wssb, @tcb
    • If yes, you have to give permission to the bot... So read carfully what's above
    • If no, you don't need to give the bot the permission, the other features don't require your key
  3. Enjoy the features!
